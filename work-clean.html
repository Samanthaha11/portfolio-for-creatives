    Hey there! I’m a designer and illustrator, and on my days off, I’m a beekeeper and garden enthusiast. Whether I’m elbows-deep in a buzzing hive, garden soil, or paint, I am never afraid to get my hands dirty. And I like to approach design with the same mindset. Point me to a warm cup of coffee, sunlit window, and reliable Wi-Fi, and I’ll be set to take on the creative world. I'm eager to explore all aspects of design and to connect and learn from fellow designers, mentors, and industry professionals who share my passion for the industry.
